Gaxle (Gah-xle)


Age: 16
Gender: Male
Speices: Mayu

Uprising

Gaxle was born into the Gettle family. The Gettle being a well known, and well respected family of perfectly trained assasains, born, bred, and raised to kill without failure. Gaxle is usually most proficient with meelee weapons, but can perform just aswell with ranged weapons, usually snipers, or powerful handguns, his favorite being the Desert Eagle mark XIX, with hollow point rounds. With Gaxle's fast movement, and sharp hearing, he can usually predict his opponents movement, but he's not always perfect, he can be tricked quite easily, however, he is a fast learner.
